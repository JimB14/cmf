About American Biz Trader

Jan 30, 2017


Since Jan 2016, American Biz Trader has been in development. My inspiration was to create a website that truly served the needs of professional brokers.

Thank you for visiting this site and for reading this.

My goal was to create a public, affordable, high-quality, business-for-sale website designed for brokers only. At its launch, Jan 2017, I believe that we have succeeded, and I hope that you will agree.

The site is easy to navigate, fast and clean, without annoying ads or hidden fees.

I hope that committed professionals nationwide who share the vision that I hold will support the industry and join this website today. Together we can build a better means to unite buyers and sellers.

We believe that our approach reflects what professionals and prospective buyers want -- a straightforward, no-nonsense website committed to bringing together buyers and professional brokers.

We understand the challenges faced by professional brokers, prospective buyers and business sellers, and want to be part of a solution.

Our objective is simple: to help our members succeed. Our aim is to unite buyers with professional brokers to find the best-suited business opportunities.

Welcome to American Biz Trader. Please join today.
